Broomstick Dash

Take flight on your enchanted broomstick and navigate through challenging obstacles using the arrow keys on your keyboard.

Controls:

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the movement of the broomstick. Avoid obstacles by maneuvering your broomstick up or down.

  • Up Arrow: Move the broomstick upward
  • Down Arrow: Move the broomstick downward
  • Right Arrow: Speed up the broomstick
  • Left Arrow: Slow down the broomstick

    • Gameplay:

    The game starts with your broomstick positioned at the bottom of the screen. Obstacles will appear randomly on the screen. Fly your broomstick through gaps and openings in the obstacles to continue flying. The game ends if your broomstick collides with an obstacle or the edge of the screen.
